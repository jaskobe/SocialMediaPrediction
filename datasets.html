DATASET

The SMP dataset contains two subsets collected from Flickr (a photo sharing platform), SMP-T1 and SMP-T2, for the two particular tasks, respectively..
For each task, we split the data with time-order, resulting in 90% for training and 10% for testing.
The tables below show the statistics of SMP-T1 subset and SMP-T2 subset.
